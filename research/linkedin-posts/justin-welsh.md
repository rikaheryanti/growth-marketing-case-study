# Justin Welsh - Content Analysis

## Post 1  
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/justinwelsh_im-convinced-most-people-are-performative-activity-7451597563285147648-2hjE/  

Summary:  
Justin argues that many people act “performative” online, sharing content for validation rather than genuine expression. He emphasizes the importance of authenticity and real thinking instead of chasing engagement.

Insight:  
- Authenticity beats performative content  
- People can detect insincerity quickly  
- Strong personal brands are built on real perspectives, not trends  

Hook Analysis:  
Starts with a bold, slightly controversial statement (“I’m convinced most people are performative”) → instantly triggers curiosity and emotional reaction.
