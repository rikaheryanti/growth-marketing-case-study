## Key Insights

### 1. Amazon has the strongest data advantage
- Google → search intent  
- Facebook → engagement  
- Amazon → real purchase behavior
- This makes targeting far more accurate  

---

### 2. Streaming + commerce is merging
- Ads are no longer just awareness  
- Users can watch → click → buy instantly  
- Creates closed-loop attribution (ad → purchase)

---

### 3. Platform consolidation is happening
- Netflix, Spotify, Roku → integrated into Amazon DSP  
- Similar to Google consolidating search ads  
- Early adopters get long-term advantage  

---

### 4. First-party data wins (cookieless future)
- Third-party cookies are declining  
- Amazon owns user purchase data  
- More reliable targeting vs other platforms  

---

### 5. Programmatic advertising simplifies everything
- One platform → multiple channels  
- Automated bidding + targeting  
- Real-time optimization
