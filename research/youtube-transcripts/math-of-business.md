## Key Insights

### 1. Close Rate determines pricing
- If you close >80%, you're underpricing (3–4x)
- Ideal close rate ~30–40% = optimal pricing

### 2. LTV:CAC depends on business model
- SaaS: 3:1
- With humans: 6:1 → 12:1
- More human = need higher ratio

### 3. Rule of 100
- 100 actions/day for 100 days
- Volume > optimization in early stage

### 4. Speed matters
- Call leads within 60 seconds
- Delay = higher CAC + lower conversion
