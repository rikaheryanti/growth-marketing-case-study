## Key Insights

### 1. SEO is broader than Google
- Search happens across multiple platforms (YouTube, app stores, Reddit, etc.)
- Ranking outside Google can be easier and more targeted
- The key is identifying where your ICP is searching

---

### 2. Paid ads require strong unit economics
- Ads work only if LTV > CAC with reasonable payback period  
- Low pricing models struggle to sustain paid acquisition  
- AI can generate creatives, but fundamentals still matter  

---

### 3. Cold outreach depends on timing (signal)
- Generic outreach = spam  
- Effective outreach = right timing + right customer  
- Signal (e.g., contract renewal, behavior change) is critical  

---

### 4. Integration marketing is an underrated growth channel
- Integrations improve product + drive acquisition  
- Co-promotion is essential for success  
- Long-term compounding through partner ecosystems  

---

### 5. Content marketing has two paths
- Virality (short-term spikes)  
- Audience building (long-term growth)  
- Most SaaS companies benefit from steady audience building  

---

### 6. AI amplifies execution, not strategy
- AI helps with speed (content, ads, outreach)  
- But increases competition and noise  
- Core strategy still determines success  

---

### 7. Growth depends on prioritization
- Focus on 1–2 channels that work  
- Use framework: speed, cost, scalability  
- Balance short-term and long-term channels
